User Profile: Student

Managing group projects is a critical part of student life.

Students need an affordable tool to track results of group project meeting, track team attendance and manage action items.

Meetable offers a way to effectively manage teams and share meeting results and collaborate seemlessly

As a professional, being able to keep track of your conversations with clients is critical to ensure the success of projects and maintain good relationships with your clients

Keep track of your ongoing projects and the conversations you have with your clients. Send updates on what you have discussed and share outstanding action items
