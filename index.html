Be the new office rockstar

Meetable is an Saas Platform that allows for seamless and collaborative meeting note taking; tracking of action items, decisions, updates, agenda and attendees; all the while connected to the industry leading PMP and collaboration tools.

User Profile: Student

Managing group projects is a critical part of student life.

Students need an affordable tool to track results of group project meeting, track team attendance and manage action items.

Meetable offers a way to effectively manage teams and share meeting results and collaborate seemlessly

User Profile: Professional

As a professional, being able to keep track of your conversations with clients is critical to ensure the success of projects and maintain good relationships with your clients

Keep track of your ongoing projects and the conversations you have with your clients. Send updates on what you have discussed and share outstanding action items

Meetable offers a solution to keep your clients up to date on the status of your projects

User Profile: Business

In the business environment, it is important for team to keep track of meeting to ensure a streamlined flow of business

Manage organizational projects and keep track of discussions and action items to ensure clarity of execution and maintain team synergy

The Meetable platform allows businesses to efficiently operationalize strategies while maintaining open channels of communication and collaboration for project teams
